News

China announces ban on ivory trade by end of 2017

China has announced a ban on all ivory trade and processing activities by the end of 2017. Conservation groups hailed the decision as "historic" and a "game-changer" for the future of elephants.

In Tanzania’s Tarangire National Park, the population of elephants has doubled in 20 years

Conservation efforts are having a powerful impact thanks to strong national park law enforcement and grassroots efforts by conservation partners in the area.

Hong Kong pledges a total ban on ivory

Hong Kong is considered the world’s largest retail market for ivory, but recent efforts by city authorities to crack down on illegal trade are starting to make a difference.

U.S.-China Deal to Ban Ivory Trade

United States and China will work together to enact “nearly complete bans” on the import and export of ivory represents the most significant step yet in efforts to shut down an industry that has fueled the illegal hunting of elephants.
